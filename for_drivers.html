AutoHub

Lets Bargain Digitally!!!!

In summary, AutoHub is a platform where VIT students may digitally negotiate with auto rickshaw drivers. You can then select a pricing range when the interface has determined the minimum fare between two points, which we'll do by speaking with the nearby taxi drivers. All of the drivers of the vehicles who are there at that time will start bidding then they will choose provide the fairest deal they can offer. As soon as the platform determines the best price during that time window, the customer will be placed in touch with the auto driver who made the best offer.
