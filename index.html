AutoHub

Lets Bargain Digitally!!!!

AutoHub lets VIT students negotiate with autodrivers online! After consulting local taxi drivers around our school, the interface will establish a minimum rate from one site to another. You can then choose a pricing range. All available vehicle drivers will bid or say they will offer the greatest price. After the platform finds the best pricing, the vehicle driver who made the best offer will contact the consumer.
